How to Build a Gaming PC

Where Gaming Dreams Come True

People who love to game should be able to know how to build their Gaming PC because It allows them to customize how they want their gaming PC to look and provide a better gaming experience.

Steps

  • Prep 1: Tools
  • Prep 2: Gaming PC Cases
  • Prep 3: Gaming PC Parts
  • Assembly

Prep 1: Tools

Before starting your build, you will need some tools to ensure that you have the right tools and a nice workplace for the building process to go smoothly.

Workplace: You will need a large surface to work on, such as a table or floor. If you do work on the floor, make sure it is on an uncarpeted surface

Screwdrivers: You will need a Phillips #2 screwdriver and a Phillips #0 screwdriver if you install an M.2 device.

USB flash drive: You will need an 8GB flash drive or larger to store the installer for the operating system that you will use.

Prep 2: Gaming PC Cases

Before you start picking the parts for the PC, you should have a case in mind that you want to use. There are three sizes of cases: Full-tower, mid-tower, and mini-tower.
